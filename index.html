Every mini is created with care and attention to detail using wool felt, a needle, and sometimes even polymer clay.

The Process

Send me a picture of your best friend or favorite character and I will work off of that!

Depending on the complexity of the figure this step can take anywhere from 3 weeks to 3 months.

Voila! Simple as that! I have made felted mini figures, holiday ornaments, pins, and even attached to small sized chalkboards!

About me

I live in Portland, OR USA with my husband, 3 dogs, and 2 cats. I love dogs! I also have an artistic side and wanted to share my creative skill of needle felting with others. I have enjoyed creating each and every one of my felted miniatures, and I look forward to working with you too!
